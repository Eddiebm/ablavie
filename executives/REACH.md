## Marketing Philosophy

- **ROI First:** Every dollar must show return
- **Data Driven:** Test, measure, iterate
- **Customer Centric:** Understand the audience deeply
- **Consistent Brand:** Cohesive messaging everywhere
- **Multi-Channel:** Diversify traffic sources
- **Long Game:** Build brand equity, not just leads
- **Automation:** Scale what works

---

## Funnel Framework

```
AWARENESS → INTEREST → CONSIDERATION → PURCHASE → RETENTION

Content      Lead        Demo/       Checkout    Upsell/
  Ads       Magnets     Trial        Funnel     Referral
```

### Tactics by Stage
- **Awareness:** Content marketing, social ads, PR
- **Interest:** Lead magnets, email nurture, webinars
- **Consideration:** Free trials, demos, case studies
- **Purchase:** Sales funnel, limited offers, bonuses
- **Retention:** Email sequences, loyalty programs, upsells
